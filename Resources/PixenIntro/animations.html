Animations

Pixen also makes creating animations easy. Simply select New Animation from the File menu to get started. The document window is split into two sections: a filmstrip and the typical image editor. You can edit the image per usual using all of Pixen's features.

The filmstrip contains your animation's frames. Reorder and delete frames at will, or even drag cels between filmstrips. To change the duration of a frame, double-click the timestamp beneath it.

To play back your animation, simply open the Preview window.

Pixen can also import sequences of images and open animated GIFs; it can output animated GIFs, image sequences, QuickTime movies, and even sprite sheets!

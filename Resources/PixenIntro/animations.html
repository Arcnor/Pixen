Animations

Simply select New Animation from the File menu to get started. The document view is split into two sections: a filmstrip and the typical image editor. You can edit the image per usual using all of Pixen's features.

The filmstrip contains all of your frames, as well as a preview, which constantly plays back your animation. Reorder and delete frames at will, or even drag cels between filmstrips.

Pixen can read pxa files, sequences of images, and animated gifs; it can output pxa files, animated gifs, image sequences, QuickTime movies, and even sprite sheets!
